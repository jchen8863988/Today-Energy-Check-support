Today Energy Check — Support

Today Energy Check helps you see what kind of day you’re looking at: a simple score, a short takeaway, and quick taps for how you feel. Built for everyday planning (including after dialysis days). Not for diagnosis, treatment, or emergencies.

We usually reply within a few business days. For medical emergencies, contact your care team or local emergency services—not this inbox.

What’s in the app

  • Home (Today): Dialysis yesterday?, fatigue, nausea, dizziness, optional weight (kg), then save to update today’s score. On the free plan you can save once per day from this screen (you can adjust symptoms and see the score update before that final save).
  • Trend: Charts from saved scores — free: last 7 days; Pro: last 30 days and longer history in the app.
  • Log: Quick daily entry with the same feelings and optional weight.
  • Share: Share a simple “today” card via the system share sheet after you’ve saved for today.
  • Settings: Language (English / 简体中文), subscription & restore, export or delete local data, disclaimer.

Data & privacy

Entries are stored on your device by default. In Settings you can export JSON or delete all local data.
Privacy policy: jchen8863988.github.io/today-energy-check-legal

Subscriptions (Pro)

Pro may include longer history, pattern notes, and other features shown in the app. Payments go through Apple App Store and/or Google Play. Manage or cancel in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions (iOS) or Google Play → Payments & subscriptions (Android). Use Restore purchases in the app after reinstalling or on a new device (same store account).

Troubleshooting

  • Can’t save / errors: Free up storage; try again. If it persists, email us your device model and OS version.
  • Pro not unlocking: Use the same Apple ID / Google account you used to buy; wait a few minutes; tap Restore purchases; check subscription status in store settings.
  • Wrong language: Open in-app Settings and choose English or 中文 (简体) at the top.
